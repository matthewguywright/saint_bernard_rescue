Application Received

Your application has been received. Please read the following information. We will be in contact with you as soon as possible.

The Foster Care

If accepted, you will provide the Foster Care in your home at the provided address, segregated from your own dogs or other foster dogs you are fostering if necessary, subject to the terms and conditions of the Agreement. Your Foster Care for the dogs will be comprised of the following:

  • Providing daily wholesome dog food and water.
  • Providing a clean, grassy area for elimination.
  • Providing outdoor exercise of not less than 1 hour per day.
  • Bottle feeding for puppies, if necessary.
  • Providing a warm (in winter), or cool (in summer), safe place inside your home.
  • Administering medicines, if any
  • Transporting the dogs to the veterinarian, if necessary.
  • Socializing and playing with the dogs.

Costs and Expenses

We will pay all veterinary treatment costs that may be incurred for the dogs during Foster Care: provided that we have given you prior approval by telephone for such treatments, except in the even of a medical emergency, in which case you will use your best judgement in the matter. You will guve us the receipts from the veterinarian for the veterinary care and the medicines. Unless otherwise noted below, you will provide the following items for the dogs: dog food and/or puppy formula, dog bed, food bowls, towels, blankets, crates, cages, etc...

No Liability

You are not liable to us for any injuries to, illness or disappearance of the dogs arising out of the Foster Care, except if such injuries, illness, or disappearance are caused by or arise out of gross negligence or intentional misconduct. We are not liable for any bodily injury or property damage, losses, or injuries whatsoever to you or other persons, or to you or another person's animals or pets, caused by the actions, behavior or health of the dogs, or arising out of the Foster Care, except if such damage, losses or injuries are caused by our gross negligence or intentional misconduct.

Returning Dogs

You agree to contact us immediately by telephone in the event that you are no longer able to provide Foster Care to any or all of the dogs. You will continue to care for the dogs for a reasonable period of time, until we are able to come to your home to retrieve any dogs being returned, or until we can accept the return of the dogs from you at our facilities. We will notify you by telephone whenever we wish to retrieve any or all of the dogs, whether for adoption into a permanent home or any other reason. Tou will cooperate with us by promptly arranging a mutually satisfying time for us to pick up any dogs being returned. You may not entrust care of the dogs to any other persons or relocate the dogs.

Adoption by Caretaker

In the event that you wish to adopt any or all of the dogs as household pet(s) for yourself, you must first enter into an Adoption Agreement, and if the dog(s) is not sterilized, also enter into our Sterilization Agreement.

Loaned Equipment and Supplies

You will return loaned items to us in good, clean condition (subject to normal wear and tear) at the end of the foster care period, or whenever we request them. If the foregoing items are lost, stolen, or damaged beyond normal wear and tear as a result of your negligence or misconduct, you will promptly pay us the replacement price to purchase new items.

Special Sterilization Clause

This clause if for Foster Care providers, who are fostering a puppy, that is not of the age or weight appropriate for teh sterilization procedure. It is intent of the Foster Care providers to adopt the puppy. The puppy is placed in their care under this Foster Care Agreement, until the time comes for the sterilization procedure. The procedure will be paid for by the Rescue Foundation. When the procedure has been completed, the Foster Care Providers will enter into the normal Adopters Contract.
